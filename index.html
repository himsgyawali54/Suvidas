\ Planning \

Our Process

01

Identify service requirement

We initiate the process by determining the service needs of our clients, whether it be financial support or strategies for company growth. Based on this information, we tailor our approach and proceed accordingly. By clearly understanding our client's requirements, we can ensure that we provide the most appropriate and effective service.

02

Gathering Information

To incline a company's market sharply, we understand its vision, goals, and missions. We also analyze and identify the company's current challenges, weaknesses, or areas for improvement. In case of a company's downfall, we collaborate with owners or shareholders to make a strategy and take the company to a new higher level.

03

Planning and Prioritization

We mainly make short-term and long-term plans according to the company's business line graph and develop a roadmap to achieve the prioritized initiatives. We also lead businesses on the right path to make them relevant and effective by monitoring progress and adjusting plans regularly.

04

Task Delineation

Our company offers various services, including marketing, branding, sales, market measurement, and evaluation. These services aim to improve a company's revenue, drive growth, increase visibility, and expand reach. A company can increase its success and reach its goals by utilizing our services.

05

Implementation

To ensure effective implementation, we establish a clear timeline. This timeline details each task and sets deadlines and milestones. Evaluating progress against set goals helps us measure success. We can guarantee a smooth and successful implementation by having a timeline and regularly assessing progress.

\ Process \

Our Selection Process

Suvedas reviews and screens a large number of investment proposals, conducts thorough due diligence, and negotiates and finalizes the terms of successful investments. Suvedas also provideson going support to its portfolio companies to help them grow and succeed

01

Application and Screening

The first step is to collect an application, which includes information about startups’ business idea, market opportunity, and team. We screen the applications to determine which startups meet our criteria and are a good fit for our program.

02

Interviews and Business Plan Review

After proper screening we conduct in-person or virtual interviews with the startups and review their business plans and financial projections to determine their potential for growth and scalability.

03

Team Assessment

We assess the strengths and weaknesses of the startups' teams, including their experience, skills, and motivation. We also assess the team's ability to communicate and collaboration efficiency.

04

Final Selection

Based on the information gathered through the application, interviews, business plan review, team assessment, and market analysis, our team makes a final selection of the startups we will work with.
